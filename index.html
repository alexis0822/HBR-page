Services

Professional athletic training services tailored to your needs:

Athletic Assessments

Personalized analysis to identify muscle imbalances, weaknesses, and areas for improvement in physical performance, aimed at athletes and active individuals.

Passive Stretching

Manual technique where the therapist mobilizes the client's joints and muscles to improve flexibility and reduce accumulated tension.

Rehab / Recovery / Modalities

Personalized physical rehabilitation and muscle recovery sessions using advanced techniques and tools tailored to the client's needs.

Manual Therapy / Cupping

Use of manual massages combined with suction cups (cupping) to relieve muscle tension, improve circulation and reduce chronic pain.

Compression Boots

Dynamic air compression is used to apply pressure to the body in a rhythmic pattern, mimicking the body's natural muscle pumps. Pressure begins at the point furthest from the heart and gradually increases, promoting blood flow and lymphatic drainage.

Event Coverage

Athletic therapy and rehabilitation services to support athletes during competitions, tournaments, and other sporting events.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session includes a comprehensive assessment of your condition, medical history review, and discussion of your goals. We'll develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs.

How long are the sessions?

Initial sessions typically last 60-90 minutes to allow for thorough assessment. Follow-up sessions are usually 45-60 minutes, depending on your treatment plan.

Do I need a referral?

No referral is required to schedule an appointment. However, if you're working with other healthcare providers, we're happy to collaborate with them for optimal results.

What should I wear?

Comfortable, athletic clothing that allows for easy movement is recommended. For certain treatments, loose-fitting clothes may be more appropriate.
